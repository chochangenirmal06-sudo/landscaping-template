## FAQ

```
Label:     FAQ
Headline:  Questions We Hear
           All the Time.

Q&A:

  Q: How quickly can you start?
  A: Most projects begin within 1–2 weeks of estimate approval. For
     urgent requests, we do our best to accommodate faster timelines.

  Q: Are estimates really free?
  A: Absolutely. We come to your property, assess the space, and provide
     a detailed written quote — no cost, no obligation, no pressure.

  Q: Are you licensed and insured?
  A: Yes. GreenEdge carries $2M in general liability insurance and full
     workers' compensation. We're happy to provide certificates on request.

  Q: Do I need to be home during the work?
  A: You don't need to be home during the project, but we love doing a
     final walkthrough together to make sure you're 100% satisfied.

  Q: What areas do you serve?
  A: We serve all of Austin and surrounding communities within 30 miles,
     including Westlake Hills, Barton Creek, Cedar Park, Round Rock, and more.

  Q: Do you offer ongoing maintenance plans?
  A: Yes — we offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly maintenance programs
     tailored to your property's needs and your budget.

  Q: What if I'm not happy with the result?
  A: We make it right. Every project comes with our written satisfaction
     guarantee. We don't close the job until you're completely happy.
```
